Red milkweed beetle genome is published!!

Sep 9, 9099 1 min read

Red Milkweed beetles and their milkweed hosts are a poster child of codiversification at the insect-plant interface.

We recently sequenced and assembled a reference genome for the common red milkweed longhorn beetle (Tetraopes tetraopthalmus). Check it out!
